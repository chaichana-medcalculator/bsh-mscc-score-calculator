BSH-MSCC Score Calculator

Survival Prediction for Malignant Spinal Cord Compression
Cut-off for short-term survival prediction: Score > 18 (AUC = 0.93, Sensitivity 81.5%, Specificity 85.7%). Absolute 6-month survival probability is estimated using the Cox baseline survival function at 6 months (S₀(6 months)).
For prognostic support in MSCC; treatment decisions should be individualized and multidisciplinary.

Laboratory Parameters (Automatic NLR Calculation)

Neutrophil-to-lymphocyte ratio (NLR) = %Neutrophil / %Lymphocyte. The score model uses a dichotomized threshold of NLR > 3.6.
